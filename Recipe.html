Recipes Vegetarian Gluten-Free Desserts

1. Vegetable Stir-Fry

Tags: Vegetarian, Quick
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup broccoli
  • 1 cup bell peppers
  • 1 cup carrots
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
Instructions:

Heat olive oil in a pan, add vegetables, and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes. Add soy sauce and cook for an additional 2 minutes.

2. Lemon Garlic Chicken

Tags: Gluten-Free, Quick
Ingredients:
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:

Marinate chicken in lemon juice, garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper for 30 minutes. Grill for 6-7 minutes on each side.

3. Caprese Salad

Tags: Vegetarian, Fresh
Ingredients:
  • 3 tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 ball mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:

Layer tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil. Drizzle with balsamic vinegar and season with salt and pepper.

4. Shrimp Tacos

Tags: Quick, Seafood
Ingredients:
  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning
  • 4 corn tortillas
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1 avocado, sliced
Instructions:

Season shrimp with taco seasoning and sauté until cooked. Serve in tortillas with cabbage and avocado.

5. Quinoa Salad

Tags: Vegetarian, Healthy
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:

Mix quinoa with vegetables, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Serve chilled.

6. Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken

Tags: Gluten-Free
Ingredients:
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:

Stuff chicken breasts with spinach and feta. Sear in olive oil and bake at 375°F for 25 minutes.

7. Creamy Tomato Soup

Tags: Vegetarian, Comfort Food
Ingredients:
  • 4 cups tomatoes, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:

Sauté onion and garlic, add tomatoes and simmer. Blend and stir in cream, then season with salt and pepper.

8. Chickpea Curry

Tags: Vegan, Spicy
Ingredients:
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • Salt to taste
Instructions:

Sauté onion, add chickpeas and curry powder. Stir in coconut milk and simmer for 15 minutes. Season with salt.

9. Roasted Vegetable Medley

Tags: Vegetarian, Healthy
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (zucchini, bell peppers, carrots)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian herbs
Instructions:

Toss vegetables with olive oil, salt, and herbs. Roast at 425°F for 25-30 minutes.

10. Banana Pancakes

Tags: Vegetarian, Breakfast
Ingredients:
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • Maple syrup for serving
Instructions:

Blend all ingredients until smooth. Cook on a skillet until golden brown. Serve with maple syrup.

11. Spinach Frittata

Tags: Vegetarian, Breakfast
Ingredients:
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:

Whisk eggs, add spinach and feta. Pour into a skillet and cook until set.

12. Pesto Pasta

Tags: Vegetarian, Quick
Ingredients:
  • 8 oz pasta
  • 1/2 cup pesto
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • Salt to taste
Instructions:

Cook pasta, drain, and mix with pesto and parmesan. Serve warm.

13. Grilled Vegetable Sandwich

Tags: Vegetarian, Sandwich
Ingredients:
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 4 slices of bread
  • 1/4 cup hummus
Instructions:

Grill vegetables, spread hummus on bread, and layer with grilled veggies. Assemble sandwich.

14. Coconut Rice

Tags: Vegan, Gluten-Free
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup jasmine rice
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt to taste
Instructions:

Combine all ingredients in a pot, bring to a boil, then simmer until rice is cooked.

15. Baked Sweet Potatoes

Tags: Vegan, Healthy
Ingredients:
  • 2 sweet potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:

Rub sweet potatoes with olive oil, season, and bake at 400°F for 45 minutes.

16. Greek Yogurt Parfait

Tags: Vegetarian, Breakfast
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • 1 cup mixed berries
Instructions:

Layer yogurt, granola, and berries in a glass. Serve immediately.

17. Vegetable Curry

Tags: Vegetarian, Spicy
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • Salt to taste
Instructions:

Cook vegetables with curry powder, add coconut milk, and simmer for 15 minutes. Season with salt.

18. Zucchini Noodles

Tags: Vegan, Healthy
Ingredients:
  • 2 zucchinis
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:

Spiralize zucchinis, sauté with tomatoes and olive oil. Season to taste.

19. Avocado Toast

Tags: Vegan, Breakfast
Ingredients:
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: red pepper flakes
Instructions:

Toast bread, mash avocado, spread on toast, and season.

20. Fruit Salad

Tags: Vegan, Fresh
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup strawberries, sliced
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup kiwi, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
Instructions:

Mix all fruits in a bowl. Drizzle with honey if desired.
