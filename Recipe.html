1. Spaghetti Carbonara ★

Tags: Quick, Dinner
Ingredients:
  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt
  • 2 plump garlic cloves
  • 50g unsalted butter
Instructions:

Cook the spaghetti. Fry the pancetta. Beat the eggs and combine with cheeses. Mix everything together and serve with extra cheese and black pepper.

    2. Chicken Alfredo ★

    Tags: Dinner
    Ingredients:
    • 2 chicken breasts
    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1 tablespoon butter
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    Instructions:

    Cook chicken in a pan until golden brown. In a separate pan, melt butter, add garlic, heavy cream, and Parmesan. Mix with chicken and serve over pasta.
